Explore Apple Fest with us!

location

The Apple Harvest Festival takes place in Downtown Ithaca, New York. It happens on the Ithaca Commons pedestrian mall and its surrounding streets and parks.

Address: 171 E M.L.K. Jr. St, Ithaca, NY 14850

transportation

From the Collegetown to Ithaca Commons...

  • riding a TCAT bus takes about 10 minutes
  • walking to the festival takes about 17 minutes
  • riding a bike takes about 7 minutes

information

On the last Friday, Saturday, and Sunday of September, Apple Harvest Festival hosts an event which many apple farmers and local residences bring local products, fresh baked goods, and family entertainment at every turn, games, rides, prizes and live performances on two stages.

Hours of the festival: Friday (12:00pm - 6:00pm) Saturday & Sunday (10:00am - 6:00pm). Visit the festival with you friends and family and spend some time to reflex and have fun!

About Ithaca

Is this your first time in Ithaca? Ithaca is a small city located in New York, US.

From November to February, the weather is very cold and snowy. Bring jacket or warm clothes for the late September (when the festival happens). Cornell University is located in Ithaca and the school contains a lots of slopes. Try avoiding wearing heals or uncomfortable shoes if you want to have a tour of the school.
